About: The Founder

Varsha started her professional career as an artist during her research period, where she participated in many workshops and shows. She met and saw the works of many international and national renowned artists and got encouraged by them. This helped her in evolving her art and creativity to a professional level. For more than 20 years (after Ph.D.), Varsha has gone through many such dimensions as an artist to gain profound insight to bringing a piece of art to life, an idea to canvas.

Courses Offered

Level 1:(Beginner)
Aim is to nurture a love for art that touches the hearts and souls.

Level 2:(Basic)
To teach proper use of brushes , colour mixing using water colour, acrylic and pastels.

Level 3:(Intermediate I)
To express themselves and explain their ideas to others.

Level 4:(Intermediate II)
Learn various modern drawing and painting techniques.

Level 5:(Higher)
To increase observation skills and challenge you to work in a variety of media. Draw from life or imgination. Discuss about the techiniques from old master's works like Picasso and Seurat.

Level 6:(Professional)
Design school preparation. Portfolio presentation and rendering techniques.
